One particular book which is recommended reading is The Street Lawyer by John Grisham. This book is about a lawyer who begins re-evaluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occours within his law firm . Consequently, he becomes acquainted with the inner city streets , and realizes the harsh exitstence of the homeless, and vows to give them a chance in the cousts. The Street Lawyer is a great book. It is well written and interesting.Other books by John Grisham include The Firm. The Pelican Brief. and The Client.
